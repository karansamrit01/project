Simple-Panipuri

Price--100Rs

Ingredients:

  • pack store-bought puris
  • Boiled, mashed potatoes and chickpeas
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • Ginger and green chillies
  • Tamarind paste and black salt
  • Water


Steps:
  1. For the filling: Mash boiled potatoes and chickpeas. Mix in black salt and chaat masala.
  2. For the pani: Blend mint, coriander, green chillies, ginger, and tamarind paste with cold water. Add black salt to taste.
  3. For assembly: Gently crack a hole in each puri.
  4. Enjoy: Fill with the potato mixture, dip in the pani, and eat immediately.
