Bhel-Puri

Price--100Rs

Ingredients:

  • Puffed rice (murmura)
  • Sev
  • Papdi (crushed)
  • Boiled potato (chopped)
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Tomato (finely chopped)
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Salt â€“ as needed
  • Green chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
Steps:
  1. Take puffed rice in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add chopped potato, onion, and tomato.
  3. Mix in green chutney and tamarind chutney.
  4. 4. Sprinkle salt and chaat masala.
  5. 5. Add sev and coriander leaves.
  6. Squeeze lemon juice.
  7. Mix well and serve immediately.
